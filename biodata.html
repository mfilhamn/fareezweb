This is my Biodata

I was born on 14 June 2004 at Hospital Kota Bharu, Kelantan. I am originally from Kok Lanas, Kelantan, where I was raised with my loving family, my parents, three brothers, and a younger sister.

I began my education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Pangkal Kalong, followed by my secondary studies at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Pangkal Kalong. After completing my SPM, I worked for around three months at McDonald's USJ1, Subang Jaya, Selangor, while waiting for my UPU results. This experience helped build my communication skills and confidence in handling people from different backgrounds.

I am now in my final year of a Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Machang. This course, coded IMD318, focuses on practical web development skills and digital content creation. It teaches students how to design, build, and publish websites using HTML and CSS.

Through this subject, I have gained valuable skills in designing responsive layouts, organizing content structure, and adding engaging elements like animations, image galleries, and navigation menus. IMD318 has truly allowed me to express creativity while learning a vital digital skill for the future.

I consider myself a passionate, hardworking, and curious learner who is always open to new experiences. Outside of academics, I enjoy exploring nature through hiking, capturing moments through photography, and spending quality time with my friends and family. My dreams include becoming a skilled information professional, traveling the world, and one day contributing to a tech-driven society.
